Hash Inspector

A hash function maps any input to a fixed-length digest. A one-character change produces a completely different hash (avalanche effect). Hashes are one-way — you cannot reverse them to get the input.

Quick test:
{{if .Hashes}}
{{range $name, $hash := .Hashes}}

{{$hash}}
{{end}}
